Stars Through the Rain, book written by Miralda Reyes

"Although in recent years my life has been permeated by Doug's tragedy, I still enjoy the smell of the sea at night and the phosphorescence that appears when the water hits the stones. It looks like falling stars."

—Stars Through the Rain


Growing as a human being

About that time, Miralda fell down the stairs and broke three bones in one leg. In that state, she had to face total desolation and devastating internal conflicts. Despite her appalling circumstances, she didn't give up. She managed to take care of Doug and move the economic situation forward while continuing with her art and meditation, which have been her refuge and salvation.—


Pointing at the stars

"Last night we went out to the middle of the road to see the stars. I can see the silhouettes of the mountains, and the trees lit by the crescent moon. A light breeze is moving toward us, and Doug hugs me from behind. I put my head on his chest, and we both look up at the sky that seems so close. I have not seen it for so long ... As soon as I return to Vancouver I will try to paint tonight on a canvas. I put my head on his chest and we both looked up at the sky that seems so close. I haven't seen it for so long! I admire him for a few seconds and I am touched by such beauty. But I notice that some stars seem to be splintered, like us. —What is your star? — he asks me. That one, I reply, pointing at random, following his game. I like it, he says, it's different from all the others. I look at her. Yes, it is different. It is splintered."


—Stars Through the Rain.—


Based on a true story

Miralda arrived in Canada after fleeing from an economic disaster in her country, with the hope of improving her life. After a few years in Canada she faced a tragedy involving Doug, her partner


My opportunity to give

The road to her recovery has been long and difficult, but progress has been made. Miralda set out to write a book so that Doug's tragedy would not be in vain.At least,it would vindicate him while at the same time serving all those who want to hear his story.
