I live in Vancouver, BC



Out of my window, I can see the view of the astonishing Simon Fraser river. It is the longest river within British Columbia, Canada. Many poems have been written about this river.

"I’d love to hear from you. Please leave comment below …







Walking along Fraser river

Come and walk with me along the Fraser River! It is a wonderful experience. The Riverfront walk is located south of South East Marine Drive, between Kerr Street and Argyle Street, Vancouver, BC. The bulrush reeds and long grass at the edge of the riverbank provide an excellent habitat for waterfowl, and spawning salmon.

Kitsilano beach

The Kitsilano neighbourhood in Vancouver is a lovely area that is known for its beautiful beach. The atmosphere is relaxed, despite it being a bustling beach with lots of activity. Although is a little bit far from home (35 min), it is my favourite beach. I love it, specially when I write or paint.


My office

"Summer is one of the times of the year that I like most in Vancouver. The days are longer and full of life, everything resurfaces and choruses with a burning, contagious rhythm. People take off their shirts and throw themselves into the grass to enjoy the sun. Bicycles and skates roll by the beach, and I lay down in the midst of that flow. Upon arriving at the beach, I head toward the shadow of my favourite spot, a monumental pine tree I named my office years ago."

—Stars Through the Rain.—
